Guide

Welcome to Road of Glory, prepare for battle!

Your mission is to build towers which will defend the village.


✦ You'll start with 100 coins.

✦ You'll have to choose the tower you want to place.

✦ You can build your tower only on marked spots on the grass (Small rectangles).

✦ For each killed monster you'll receive a random amount of coins.

✦ Reach a highest score and compare it with others.

✦ Each wave the monsters will be stronger and faster.

✦ Survive 20 waves!!

✦ You have only 5 hearts, DON'T LOSE THEM!
